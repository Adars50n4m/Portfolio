# How to Add Your Videos

1.  Open the `public/videos/` folder in your project (`/Volumes/Adarsh/Portfolio/public/videos/`).
2.  Copy your video files (from your Google Drive download) into this folder.
3.  Rename them to match the code, or update `src/components/Portfolio.jsx` to match your filenames:
    - `clip1.mp4`
    - `clip2.mp4`
    - `clip3.mp4`
    - `clip4.mp4`
    - `clip5.mp4`
    - `clip6.mp4`

The portfolio will automatically play these videos when you hover over the cards.
